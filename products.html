Selecting the Right Products

Choosing the right waste management products depends on several factors:

For Residential Properties

  • Consider yard size and pet count
  • Choose receptacles that match your landscaping
  • Select bag sizes appropriate for your pets
  • Consider weather resistance for outdoor storage

For Commercial Properties

  • Assess traffic volume and property size
  • Plan for multiple station locations
  • Consider maintenance requirements
  • Evaluate durability for high-use areas
  • Plan for bag supply and replacement schedules
