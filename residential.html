Keep Your Yard Clean and Safe

As a pet owner, you know how quickly waste can accumulate in your yard. Regular cleanup is essential for maintaining a clean, safe environment for your family and pets. But let's face it—it's not the most pleasant task, and finding time can be challenging.

That's where Happy Tails comes in. Our residential waste removal service takes care of the dirty work so you can enjoy your yard without the hassle. Whether you have one dog or multiple pets, we'll keep your property clean and pristine.

Service Frequencies

We offer flexible scheduling options to fit your needs and budget. Choose the frequency that works best for you:

Weekly Service

Perfect for households with multiple pets or high-traffic yards. Weekly service ensures your yard stays consistently clean, preventing buildup and maintaining a fresh environment.

  • Most popular option
  • Best value for regular maintenance
  • Consistent, reliable service

Custom Schedule

Have specific needs? We can create a custom schedule tailored to your property, pet count, and preferences. Whether you need service every 10 days, seasonal service, or something else entirely, we'll work with you.

One-Time Service

Need a one-time cleanup? Whether you're preparing for a special event, catching up after being away, or just need a deep clean, we offer one-time service options.

Perfect for:

  • Preparing for parties or gatherings
  • Catching up after vacation
  • Seasonal deep cleaning
