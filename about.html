About Happy Tails

Dedicated to keeping your property clean and your pets happy

Our Mission

At Happy Tails, we believe that pet ownership should be a joy, not a chore. Our mission is to provide professional, reliable waste removal services that save our customers time while keeping their properties clean, safe, and enjoyable for both pets and people.

We understand that life gets busy, and cleaning up after your pets can be time-consuming and unpleasant. That's where we come in. Our experienced team handles the dirty work so you can focus on what matters most—spending quality time with your furry friends.

Our Story

Happy Tails was founded with a simple idea: pet waste removal shouldn't be a hassle. What started as a small local service has grown into a trusted name in waste management, serving thousands of satisfied customers across multiple communities.

Our journey began when our founder, frustrated by the lack of reliable and professional waste removal services, decided to create a company that would set a new standard. We've built our reputation on three core principles: reliability, professionalism, and customer satisfaction.

Today, we're proud to serve residential customers, commercial properties, and community organizations, helping them maintain clean, healthy environments for everyone to enjoy.

Our Values

Reliability

We show up when we say we will, rain or shine. Our customers can count on us to be consistent and dependable.

Professionalism

Every member of our team is trained, insured, and committed to providing the highest quality service. We treat every property with respect and care.

Customer Satisfaction

Your satisfaction is our top priority. We offer flexible service options, no long-term contracts, and a 100% satisfaction guarantee.

Environmental Responsibility

We're committed to environmentally responsible waste disposal practices, ensuring that pet waste is handled and disposed of properly.

Community Focus

We're not just a service provider—we're part of your community. We support local initiatives and are invested in making our neighborhoods better places to live.

Why Choose Us?

  • Convenience: Flexible scheduling that works with your lifestyle
